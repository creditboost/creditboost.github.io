First America Credit Union’s Seasonal Promotions

Home / Blog / Blog Details

The world is evolving at a rapid pace, and so are the financial challenges we face. From inflation and rising interest rates to the growing importance of sustainable investing, staying ahead requires smart financial strategies. At First America Credit Union, we understand these challenges and are committed to helping our members navigate them with confidence. That’s why our seasonal promotions are designed to provide real value—whether you’re saving for the future, managing debt, or planning a major purchase.

Why Seasonal Promotions Matter in Today’s Economy

Inflation and Your Wallet

With inflation impacting everything from groceries to gas prices, every dollar counts. Our seasonal promotions offer higher interest rates on savings accounts, lower loan rates, and cashback rewards to help stretch your budget further. For example, our Summer Savings Boost promotion gives members an extra 0.50% APY on select savings accounts—a great way to combat inflation’s erosion of your purchasing power.

The Rise of Digital Banking

The shift to digital banking is here to stay, and we’re making it easier than ever to manage your money on the go. This fall, we’re rolling out a Mobile App Bonus: new users who complete three transactions via our app will receive a $50 credit. Plus, our e-Statements promotion gives members a chance to win $500 just for going paperless—helping you save while reducing environmental waste.

Exclusive Loan Offers for Major Life Events

Home Buying in a Competitive Market

The housing market remains tight, but our Fall Homebuyer Special provides competitive mortgage rates and closing cost assistance for first-time buyers. With rates still higher than in recent years, locking in a low fixed-rate mortgage now could save you thousands over the life of your loan.

Tackling Student Debt

Student loan payments have resumed, and many borrowers are feeling the pinch. Our Student Loan Refinance Promotion cuts rates by up to 1% for qualifying members, helping reduce monthly payments and freeing up cash for other priorities.

Sustainable Finance: Banking with a Purpose

Green Auto Loans

Electric vehicles (EVs) are more popular than ever, and our Green Auto Loan Promotion offers discounted rates for members financing hybrid or electric cars. Not only will you save on fuel, but you’ll also get a 0.25% rate reduction—making eco-friendly driving even more affordable.

Rewards for Sustainable Choices

We’re also rewarding members who make environmentally conscious decisions. Our Eco-Saver Account earns bonus dividends when you use our carbon footprint tracker and meet sustainability goals. It’s banking that aligns with your values.

Holiday Season Financial Wellness

Smart Spending for the Holidays

The holidays can strain budgets, but our Holiday Loan Special offers low-interest personal loans with no fees for early repayment. Plus, our Year-End CD Special provides locked-in rates for those looking to grow their savings before the new year.

Giving Back to the Community

This season, we’re matching donations made through our Charity Rewards Program—up to $10,000 in total—to support local food banks and shelters. It’s just one more way we help our members make a difference where it counts.

At First America Credit Union, we believe in empowering our members with financial tools that adapt to their needs. Whether it’s saving smarter, borrowing wisely, or banking sustainably, our seasonal promotions are here to help you thrive in an ever-changing world.
